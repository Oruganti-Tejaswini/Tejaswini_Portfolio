E-Bay Data Challenge in NERs ( In progress )

This is a Data Challenge that is conducting by Ebay helps to find the Named Entity recognition on the Data that the product list they has.

The sentences should be the input which consists of any category for example:

  • Company Name
  • Product name
  • Product Size Cola
  • Product Colour
  • Location of the Company
  • Mass
  • Type of the product
  • Price of the product
The prediction should be a kind of dictionary that the key is the category and value is the product information.


    Project Description:-

  • Extraction and cleaning the data
  • Implement the BERT model and find the parts of speech of the each sentence
  • As a result, based on the knowledge that model gains it returns the Named and Entity dictionary.
